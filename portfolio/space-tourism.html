Space Tourism Application

A educational application that let's you travel in space.

Basic-Idea

This application is a cutting-edge educational platform that transforms users into space explorers, offering immersive journeys through our solar system and beyond. This interactive application allows users to virtually travel to different planets, moons, and other celestial bodies, providing detailed information and stunning visuals about each destination.
